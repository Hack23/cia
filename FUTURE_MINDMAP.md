# 🧠 Future Mindmap Vision: 2026–2037 Roadmap

This document maps the conceptual evolution of the Citizen Intelligence Agency platform capabilities from practical 2026 enhancements through visionary 2037 transformations. The roadmap accounts for rapid AI/LLM advancement—currently leveraging Anthropic Opus 4.6 with minor updates every ~2.3 months and major version upgrades annually—while anticipating competitor models, emergent AI architectures, and the trajectory toward AGI.

## 🌈 Long-Term Vision Statement

The Citizen Intelligence Agency's capability evolution follows a clear progression driven by AI advancement:

1. **2026: Augment** — Add AI-powered analysis to the proven platform, enhancing human understanding of political data
2. **2027–2029: Intelligentize** — Transform the platform into an intelligent analytics system with natural language interaction, autonomous monitoring, and international reach
3. **2030–2033: Autonomize** — Enable increasingly autonomous intelligence gathering, analysis, and reporting with human oversight and quality assurance
4. **2034–2037: Democratize** — Evolve into essential democratic infrastructure providing personalized political intelligence to every citizen globally

The AI evolution assumption—Anthropic Opus 4.6 as baseline, minor updates every ~2.3 months, major upgrades annually, with competitors and eventual AGI—means the platform must be fundamentally **adaptive**, **provider-agnostic**, and **human-centered**, ensuring technology serves democratic values regardless of which AI systems prove most capable.
